हनुमान चालीसा

HANUMAN CHALISA

हनुमान चालीसा गोस्वामी तुलसीदास द्वारा रचित भगवान हनुमान की स्तुति में लिखी गई चालीस चौपाइयाँ हैं। इसका नित्य पाठ करने से भय, रोग और कष्ट दूर होते हैं।

Hanuman Chalisa is a devotional hymn by Goswami Tulsidas in praise of Lord Hanuman. Daily recitation removes fear, illness and hardship.

दोहा — Doha 1

श्रीगुरु चरन सरोज रज, निज मनु मुकुरु सुधारि।

बरनउँ रघुबर बिमल जसु, जो दायकु फल चारि॥

Shri Guru Charan Saroj Raj, Nij Manu Mukuru Sudhari.

Barnau Raghubar Bimal Jasu, Jo Dayaku Phal Chari.

Cleansing the mirror of my mind with the dust of the holy feet of the Guru, I narrate the pure glory of Raghubar, which bestows the four fruits of life.
दोहा — Doha 2

बुद्धिहीन तनु जानिके, सुमिरौं पवन-कुमार।

बल बुद्धि बिद्या देहु मोहिं, हरहु कलेस बिकार॥

Buddhihin Tanu Janike, Sumiron Pavan Kumar.

Bal Buddhi Vidya Dehu Mohi, Harahu Kales Bikar.

Knowing myself to be ignorant, I remember you O Son of Wind. Grant me strength, wisdom and knowledge, and remove my sorrows.

चौपाई 1–8
1

जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुन सागर।

जय कपीस तिहुँ लोक उजागर॥

Jai Hanuman Gyan Gun Sagar.

Jai Kapis Tihun Lok Ujagar.

Victory to Hanuman, the ocean of wisdom and virtue. Victory to the Lord of monkeys, illuminating the three worlds.
·
2

राम दूत अतुलित बल धामा।

अञ्जनि-पुत्र पवनसुत नामा॥

Ram Doot Atulit Bal Dhama.

Anjani Putra Pavansut Nama.

Messenger of Rama, abode of immeasurable strength. Son of Anjani, also known as the Son of the Wind.
·
3

महाबीर बिक्रम बजरंगी।

कुमति निवार सुमति के संगी॥

Mahavir Bikram Bajrangi.

Kumati Nivar Sumati Ke Sangi.

The great hero, valiant and thunderbolt-bodied. Remover of evil intellect, companion of good intellect.
·
4

कञ्चन बरन बिराज सुबेसा।

कानन कुण्डल कुञ्चित केसा॥

Kanchan Baran Biraj Subesa.

Kanan Kundal Kunchit Kesa.

Golden-hued, splendidly attired. Wearing ear-rings, with curly locks of hair.
·
5

हाथ बज्र औ ध्वजा बिराजै।

काँधे मूँज जनेऊ साजै॥

Hath Bajra Au Dhvaja Birajain.

Kandhe Munj Janeu Sajai.

Holding a thunderbolt and flag in hand. The sacred thread of munja grass adorns the shoulder.
·
6

शङ्कर सुवन केसरी नन्दन।

तेज प्रताप महा जग बन्दन॥

Shankar Suvan Kesari Nandan.

Tej Pratap Maha Jag Bandan.

Incarnation of Shiva, son of Kesari. Your glory and majesty is praised all over the world.
·
7

विद्यावान गुनी अति चातुर।

राम काज करिबे को आतुर॥

Vidyavan Guni Ati Chatur.

Ram Kaj Karibe Ko Atur.

Very learned, virtuous and supremely clever. Always eager to do the work of Rama.
·
8

प्रभु चरित्र सुनिबे को रसिया।

राम लखन सीता मन बसिया॥

Prabhu Charitra Sunibe Ko Rasiya.

Ram Lakhan Sita Man Basiya.

You love to listen to the deeds of the Lord. Rama, Laxman and Sita dwell in your heart.
Advertisement
चौपाई 9–16
9

सूक्ष्म रूप धरि सियहिं दिखावा।

बिकट रूप धरि लंक जरावा॥

Sukshma Roop Dhari Siyahi Dikhava.

Bikat Roop Dhari Lanka Jarava.

Taking a tiny form, you appeared before Sita. Taking a terrible form, you burned Lanka.
·
10

भीम रूप धरि असुर संहारे।

रामचन्द्र के काज सँवारे॥

Bhim Roop Dhari Asur Sanghare.

Ramchandra Ke Kaj Sanvare.

Taking a giant form, you slew the demons and completed all the tasks of Ramachandra.
·
11

लाय सञ्जीवन लखन जियाये।

श्रीरघुबीर हरषि उर लाये॥

Lay Sanjivan Lakhan Jiyaye.

Shri Raghuvir Harashi Ur Laye.

Bringing the Sanjivani herb, you revived Laxman. Shri Raghuvir joyfully embraced you.
·
12

रघुपति कीन्ही बहुत बड़ाई।

तुम मम प्रिय भरतहि सम भाई॥

Raghupati Kinhi Bahut Badai.

Tum Mam Priya Bharatahi Sam Bhai.

Raghupati praised you greatly: "You are as dear to me as my own brother Bharat."
·
13

सहस बदन तुम्हरो जस गावैं।

अस कहि श्रीपति कण्ठ लगावैं॥

Sahas Badan Tumharo Jas Gavain.

As Kahi Shripati Kanth Lagavain.

A thousand mouths sing your glory. Saying so, Shripati (Rama) embraced you.
·
14

सनकादिक ब्रह्मादि मुनीसा।

नारद-सारद सहित अहीसा॥

Sanakadik Brahmadi Munisa.

Narad Sarad Sahit Ahisa.

Sanaka, Brahma, Narada, Saraswati and the king of serpents all sing your praise.
·
15

जम कुबेर दिगपाल जहाँ ते।

कबि कोबिद कहि सके कहाँ ते॥

Yam Kuber Digpal Jahan Te.

Kabi Kobid Kahi Sake Kahan Te.

Yama, Kubera and the guardians of the four quarters — what poet can fully describe your glory?
·
16

तुम उपकार सुग्रीवहिं कीन्हा।

राम मिलाय राज पद दीन्हा॥

Tum Upkar Sugrivehi Kinha.

Ram Milay Raj Pad Dinha.

You did a great favour to Sugriva by uniting him with Rama and restoring his kingdom.
चौपाई 17–24
17

तुम्हरो मन्त्र बिभीषन माना।

लङ्केश्वर भए सब जग जाना॥

Tumharo Mantra Vibhishan Mana.

Lankeshwar Bhaye Sab Jag Jana.

Vibhishana heeded your counsel and became the lord of Lanka — the whole world knows this.
·
18

जुग सहस्र जोजन पर भानू।

लील्यो ताहि मधुर फल जानू॥

Jug Sahastra Jojan Par Bhanu.

Lilyo Tahi Madhur Phal Janu.

The sun is thousands of yugas away — you swallowed it, thinking it to be a sweet fruit.
·
19

प्रभु मुद्रिका मेलि मुख माहीं।

जलधि लाँघि गये अचरज नाहीं॥

Prabhu Mudrika Meli Mukh Mahin.

Jaladhi Langhi Gaye Acharaj Nahin.

Carrying the Lord's ring in your mouth, you leaped across the ocean — no wonder!
·
20

दुर्गम काज जगत के जेते।

सुगम अनुग्रह तुम्हरे तेते॥

Durgam Kaj Jagat Ke Jete.

Sugam Anugraha Tumhare Tete.

All difficult tasks in the world become easy through your grace.
·
21

राम दुआरे तुम रखवारे।

होत न आज्ञा बिनु पैसारे॥

Ram Duare Tum Rakhvare.

Hot Na Agya Binu Paisare.

You are the guardian at the gate of Rama's abode. None may enter without your permission.
·
22

सब सुख लहै तुम्हारी सरना।

तुम रक्षक काहू को डर ना॥

Sab Sukh Lahai Tumhari Sarna.

Tum Rakshak Kahu Ko Dar Na.

All happiness is obtained by taking refuge in you. When you are the protector, there is no fear.
·
23

आपन तेज सम्हारो आपै।

तीनों लोक हाँक तें काँपै॥

Aapan Tej Samharo Aapai.

Tinon Lok Hank Te Kampai.

You alone can control your own might. All three worlds tremble at your roar.
·
24

भूत पिसाच निकट नहिं आवै।

महाबीर जब नाम सुनावै॥

Bhoot Pisach Nikat Nahin Avai.

Mahavir Jab Nam Sunavai.

Ghosts and evil spirits do not come near when the name of Mahavir is uttered.
Advertisement
चौपाई 25–32
25

नासै रोग हरै सब पीरा।

जपत निरन्तर हनुमत बीरा॥

Nasei Rog Harai Sab Pira.

Japat Nirantar Hanumat Bira.

All diseases are cured and all pain removed by constantly chanting the name of Hanuman.
·
26

सङ्कट तें हनुमान छुड़ावै।

मन क्रम बचन ध्यान जो लावै॥

Sankat Te Hanuman Chhudavai.

Man Kram Bachan Dhyan Jo Lavai.

Hanuman liberates one from all troubles who meditates on him in thought, deed and word.
·
27

सब पर राम तपस्वी राजा।

तिन के काज सकल तुम साजा॥

Sab Par Ram Tapasvi Raja.

Tin Ke Kaj Sakal Tum Saja.

Rama, the ascetic king, reigns above all. You accomplish all of his tasks.
·
28

और मनोरथ जो कोई लावै।

सोई अमित जीवन फल पावै॥

Aur Manorath Jo Koi Lavai.

Soi Amit Jivan Phal Pavai.

Whoever comes to you with any desire attains the boundless fruit of life.
·
29

चारों जुग परताप तुम्हारा।

है परसिद्ध जगत उजियारा॥

Charon Jug Partap Tumhara.

Hai Parsiddha Jagat Ujiyara.

Your glory prevails across all four ages. Your fame illuminates the entire world.
·
30

साधु-सन्त के तुम रखवारे।

असुर निकन्दन राम दुलारे॥

Sadhu Sant Ke Tum Rakhvare.

Asur Nikandan Ram Dulare.

You are the protector of saints, destroyer of demons, and dearly beloved of Rama.
·
31

अष्टसिद्धि नौ निधि के दाता।

अस बर दीन जानकी माता॥

Ashta Siddhi Nau Nidhi Ke Data.

As Bar Din Janaki Mata.

You are the granter of the eight powers and nine treasures — this boon was given by Mother Sita.
·
32

राम रसायन तुम्हरे पासा।

सदा रहो रघुपति के दासा॥

Ram Rasayan Tumhare Pasa.

Sada Raho Raghupati Ke Dasa.

You hold the essence of devotion to Rama. May you remain ever the servant of Raghupati.
चौपाई 33–40
33

तुम्हरे भजन राम को पावै।

जनम-जनम के दुख बिसरावै॥

Tumhare Bhajan Ram Ko Pavai.

Janam Janam Ke Dukh Bisravai.

By singing your praises one attains Rama and forgets the sorrows of countless lifetimes.
·
34

अन्त काल रघुबर पुर जाई।

जहाँ जन्म हरि-भक्त कहाई॥

Ant Kal Raghubar Pur Jai.

Jahan Janam Hari Bhakt Kahai.

At the end of life one goes to Raghubar's abode and is reborn as a devotee of Hari.
·
35

और देवता चित्त न धरई।

हनुमत सेइ सर्ब सुख करई॥

Aur Devata Chitt Na Dharai.

Hanumat Sei Sarb Sukh Karai.

One need not keep any other deity in mind — serving Hanuman alone gives all happiness.
·
36

सङ्कट कटै मिटै सब पीरा।

जो सुमिरै हनुमत बलबीरा॥

Sankat Katai Mitai Sab Pira.

Jo Sumirai Hanumat Balvira.

All troubles are cut away and all pain removed for whoever remembers the mighty Hanuman.
·
37

जय जय जय हनुमान गोसाईं।

कृपा करहु गुरुदेव की नाईं॥

Jai Jai Jai Hanuman Gosai.

Kripa Karahu Gurudev Ki Nai.

Victory, victory, victory to you O Hanuman! Please bless me as a Guru does his disciple.
·
38

जो सत बार पाठ कर कोई।

छूटहि बन्दि महा सुख होई॥

Jo Sat Bar Path Kar Koi.

Chhutehi Bandi Maha Sukh Hoi.

Whoever recites this a hundred times is freed from bondage and attains great happiness.
·
39

जो यह पढ़ै हनुमान चालीसा।

होय सिद्धि साखी गौरीसा॥

Jo Yeh Padhai Hanuman Chalisa.

Hoy Siddhi Sakhi Gaurisa.

Whoever reads this Hanuman Chalisa attains perfection — Lord Shiva himself is witness.
·
40

तुलसीदास सदा हरि चेरा।

कीजै नाथ हृदय मँह डेरा॥

Tulsidas Sada Hari Chera.

Kijai Nath Hriday Mah Dera.

Tulsidas is always a servant of Hari. O Lord, please make your abode in my heart.
दोहा — Closing Doha

पवनतनय सङ्कट हरन, मङ्गल मूरति रूप।

राम लखन सीता सहित, हृदय बसहु सुर भूप॥

Pavantanay Sankat Haran, Mangal Murati Roop.

Ram Lakhan Sita Sahit, Hriday Basahu Sur Bhoop.

O Son of Wind, remover of troubles, embodiment of auspiciousness — dwell in my heart along with Rama, Laxman and Sita, O King of Gods.
