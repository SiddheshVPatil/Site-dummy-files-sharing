हनुमान आरती

HANUMAN AARTI

हनुमान आरती भगवान हनुमान की पूजा के अंत में गाई जाती है। मंगलवार और शनिवार को हनुमान जी की आरती करने से विशेष फल प्राप्त होता है। यह भक्तों के सभी कष्टों को दूर करती है।

Hanuman Aarti is sung at the end of Lord Hanuman's worship. Performing Hanuman's aarti on Tuesdays and Saturdays gives special results. It removes all troubles of the devotees.

आरती कीजे हनुमान लला की।

दुष्ट दलन रघुनाथ कला की॥

Aarti Kije Hanuman Lala Ki.

Dusht Dalan Raghunath Kala Ki.

Perform the aarti of the beloved Hanuman. He is the destroyer of the wicked, the embodiment of the art of Raghunath (Rama).

संतन के प्रभु सदा सहाई॥

Jake Bal Se Girivar Kampe.

Rog Dosh Jake Nikat Na Jhanke.

Anjani Putra Maha Baldai.

Santan Ke Prabhu Sada Sahai.

By whose strength the great mountains tremble. Near whom disease and fault dare not approach. Son of Anjani, the great giver of strength. Lord of the saints, always their helper.

लंका जारि सिया सुधि लाए॥

लंका सो कोट समुद्र सी खाई।

जात पवनसुत बार न लाई॥

De Vira Raghunath Pathaye.

Lanka Jari Siya Sudhi Laye.

Lanka So Kot Samudra Si Khai.

Jat Pavansut Bar Na Lai.

The brave one sent by Raghunath. He burned Lanka and brought news of Sita. Lanka had walls like a fort and a moat like the ocean. Yet Pavansut (Hanuman) crossed without a moment's delay.

Lakshman Murchchhit Pade Sakare.

Aani Sanjivan Pran Ubare.

Paithi Patal Tori Jam Kare.

Ahiravan Ki Bhuja Ukhare.

Laxman lay unconscious in the morning. Bringing Sanjivani, Hanuman saved his life. Entering the underworld, he broke the chains of Yama. He uprooted the arms of Ahiravana.

सुर नर मुनि जन आरती उतारें।

Bai Bhuja Asur Dal Mare.

Dahini Bhuj Santjan Tare.

Sur Nar Muni Jan Aarti Utaren.

Jai Jai Jai Hanuman Ucharen.

With the left arm he slays the armies of demons. With the right arm he saves the saintly devotees. Gods, men and sages all perform his aarti. Crying out: Victory, victory, victory to Hanuman!

मालपुआ अरगजा।

केसर कस्तूरी राजे॥

Kanchan Thal Viraje.

Motin Ki Mal Suhaye.

Malpua Aragaja.

Kesar Kasturi Raje.

A golden plate is placed for the offering. A garland of pearls adorns it beautifully. Malpua sweets and fragrant things. Saffron and musk shine in the offering.
