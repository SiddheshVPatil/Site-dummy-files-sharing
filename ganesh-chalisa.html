गणेश चालीसा

GANESH CHALISA

गणेश चालीसा भगवान गणेश की स्तुति में रचित चालीस चौपाइयाँ हैं। गणेश जी को सभी कार्यों के आरम्भ में सर्वप्रथम पूजा जाता है। इनकी पूजा से सभी बाधाएं दूर होती हैं।

Ganesh Chalisa is a devotional hymn of forty verses in praise of Lord Ganesha. Ganesha is worshipped first before all auspicious beginnings. His worship removes all obstacles.

दोहा — Doha

जय गणपति सदगुण सदन, कविवर बदन कृपाल।

विघ्न हरण मंगल करण, जय जय गिरिजालाल॥

Jai Ganapati Sadgun Sadan, Kavivar Badan Kripal.

Vighna Haran Mangal Karan, Jai Jai Girijal.

Victory to Ganapati, abode of virtues, with the face of the king of poets, merciful. Remover of obstacles, creator of auspiciousness — victory to the son of Girija (Parvati).

चौपाई 1–8
1

जय जय जय गणपति गणराजू।

मंगल भरण करण शुभ काजू॥

Jai Jai Jai Ganapati Ganraju.

Mangal Bharan Karan Shubh Kaju.

Victory, victory, victory to Ganapati, the king of hosts. He who fills life with auspiciousness and accomplishes blessed deeds.
·
2

जय गजबदन सदन सुखदाता।

विश्व विनायक बुद्धि विधाता॥

Jai Gajbadan Sadan Sukhdaata.

Vishwa Vinayak Buddhi Vidhata.

Victory to the elephant-faced one, the giver of happiness. The Vinayaka of the universe, the bestower of intellect.
·
3

वक्रतुण्ड शुचि शुण्ड सुहावन।

तिलक त्रिपुण्ड भाल मन भावन॥

Vakratund Shuchi Shund Suhavan.

Tilak Tripund Bhal Man Bhavan.

Curved-trunked, with a pure and beautiful trunk. The tilak and tripunda on the forehead pleases the mind.
·
4

राजत मणि मुक्तन उर माला।

स्वर्ण मुकुट शिर नयन विशाला॥

Rajat Mani Muktan Ur Mala.

Swarna Mukut Shir Nayan Vishala.

A necklace of gems and pearls adorns the chest. A golden crown on the head, large eyes.
·
5

पुस्तक पाणि कुठार त्रिशूलं।

मोदक भोग सुगंध फूलं॥

Pustak Pani Kuthar Trishulam.

Modak Bhog Sugandh Phulam.

Book in hand, axe and trident. Modak as offering and fragrant flowers.
·
6

सुन्दर पीताम्बर तन साजित।

चरण पादुका मुनि मन राजित॥

Sundar Pitambar Tan Sajit.

Charan Paduka Muni Man Rajit.

Beautiful yellow garment adorns the body. The sandals on his feet delight the minds of sages.
·
7

धनि शिव सुवन षडानन भ्राता।

गौरी ललन विश्वविख्याता॥

Dhani Shiv Suvan Shadanan Bhrata.

Gauri Lalan Vishvavikhyata.

Blessed son of Shiva, brother of six-faced Kartikeya. Beloved son of Gauri, renowned throughout the universe.
·
8

ऋद्धि सिद्धि तव चँवर सुधारें।

मूषक वाहन सोहत द्वारें॥

Riddhi Siddhi Tav Chanvar Sudhare.

Mushak Vahan Sohat Dvare.

Riddhi and Siddhi wave fly-whisks for you. The mouse vehicle shines at the door.
Advertisement
चौपाई 9–20
9

कहूँ जन्म शुभ कथा तुम्हारी।

अति शुचि पावन मंगलकारी॥

Kahun Janam Shubh Katha Tumhari.

Ati Shuchi Pavan Mangalkari.

I tell your auspicious birth story. It is most pure, holy and auspicious.
·
10

एक समय गिरिराज कुमारी।

पुत्र हेतु तप कीन्हो भारी॥

Ek Samay Giriraja Kumari.

Putra Hetu Tap Kinhu Bhari.

Once the daughter of the mountain king (Parvati) performed great penance for a son.
·
11

भयो यज्ञ जब पूर्ण अनूपा।

तब पहुँच्यो तुम धरि द्विज रूपा॥

Bhayo Yagya Jab Purn Anupa.

Tab Pahunchyo Tum Dhari Dvij Rupa.

When the incomparable sacrifice was completed, you arrived taking the form of a brahmin.
·
12

अतिथि जानि के गौरी सुखारी।

बहुविधि सेवा करी तुम्हारी॥

Atithi Jani Ke Gauri Sukhari.

Bahuvidi Seva Kari Tumhari.

Recognising you as a guest, Gauri was pleased. She served you in many ways.
·
13

अति प्रसन्न होकर तुम वर दीन्हा।

मातु पुत्र हित जो कुछ कीन्हा॥

Ati Prasann Hokar Tum Var Dinha.

Matu Putra Hit Jo Kuchh Kinha.

Being very pleased, you granted the boon. Whatever was done was for the mother's and son's welfare.
·
14

मिलहि पुत्र तुम बुद्धि विशाला।

बिना गर्भ धारण यहि काला॥

Milahi Putra Tum Buddhi Vishala.

Bina Garbha Dharan Yahi Kala.

You will receive a son of great wisdom at this time without bearing in the womb.
·
15

गणनायक गुण ज्ञान निधाना।

पूजित प्रथम रूप भगवाना॥

Gananayak Gun Gyan Nidhana.

Pujit Pratham Roop Bhagvana.

Leader of the ganas, treasury of virtues and wisdom. Worshipped first among all divine forms.
·
16

अस कहि अंतर्धान रूप हो।

पालना पर बालक स्वरूप हो॥

As Kahi Antardhaan Roop Ho.

Palna Par Balak Swaroop Ho.

Saying this, the form became invisible. Then appeared as a child in the cradle.
चौपाई 17–40
17

बनि शिशु रुदन जबहि तुम ठाना।

लखि मुख सुख नहिं गौरी समाना॥

Bani Shishu Rudan Jabahi Tum Thana.

Lakhi Mukh Sukh Nahin Gauri Samana.

When you as an infant began to cry, seeing your face Gauri's happiness knew no bounds.
·
18

सकल मगन सुखमंगल गावहिं।

नभ ते सुर सुमन वर्षावहिं॥

Sakal Magan Sukhmangal Gavahi.

Nabh Te Sur Suman Varshavahi.

All were absorbed in singing auspicious songs. From the sky, the gods showered flowers.
·
19

शम्भु उमा बहु दान लुटावहिं।

सुर मुनिजन सुख सागर नहावहिं॥

Shambhu Uma Bahu Dan Lutavahi.

Sur Munijan Sukh Sagar Nahavahi.

Shambhu and Uma distributed generous gifts. Gods and sages bathed in the ocean of happiness.
·
20

पूर्ण कामना रहित भक्त जो ध्यावे।

संतत संपति सकल तुम पावे॥

Purna Kamna Rahit Bhakt Jo Dhyave.

Santat Sampati Sakal Tum Pave.

The devotee who meditates on you without selfish desire — attains all wealth and prosperity continuously.
·
21–40

जो सुमिरत सिद्धि होत गणेशा।

ऋद्धि सिद्धि मिले दिन शेषा॥

संकट हरन विघ्न हरन सुखकारी।

जय जय जय गणेश विश्वविहारी॥

विद्या बुद्धि दो, सुमति प्रदाता।

विघ्नेश्वर महिमा सदा विख्याता॥

Jo Sumarat Siddhi Hot Ganesh.

Riddhi Siddhi Mile Din Shesh.

Sankat Haran Vighna Haran Sukhkari.

Jai Jai Jai Ganesh Vishvavhari.

Vidya Buddhi Do, Sumati Pradata.

Vighneshwar Mahima Sada Vikhyata.

Whoever remembers Ganesha attains perfection. Riddhi and Siddhi are obtained at the end of the day. Remover of troubles, remover of obstacles, giver of happiness. Victory, victory, victory to Ganesha who roams the universe. Grant knowledge and wisdom, O giver of good intellect. The glory of Vighneshwara is always renowned.
दोहा — Closing Doha

श्री गणेश यह चालीसा, पाठ करे धर ध्यान।

नित नव मंगल मोद भरे, पावे जो सुखधाम॥

Shri Ganesh Yeh Chalisa, Path Kare Dhar Dhyan.

Nit Nav Mangal Mod Bhare, Pave Jo Sukhdham.

Whoever recites this Shri Ganesh Chalisa with focused meditation — will always be filled with fresh auspiciousness and joy, and attain the abode of happiness.
