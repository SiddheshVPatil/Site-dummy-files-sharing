Our Purpose

BhaktiRead.in is a free online platform dedicated to making Hindu prayers, chalisa, mantras and aartis easily accessible to everyone. Our goal is simple — when you want to read a prayer, it should open instantly, look clean, and be easy to read on any device.

Whether you are at home during your morning puja, travelling, or simply want to read the Hanuman Chalisa before sleeping — BhaktiRead.in is always available, free of cost, with no login or download required.

What We Offer

We provide the full text of popular Hindu prayers in Hindi with English meaning, including:

Chalisa — Hanuman Chalisa, Durga Chalisa, Shiv Chalisa, Ganesh Chalisa, Lakshmi Chalisa and more.

Mantra — Gayatri Mantra, Mahamrityunjay Mantra, Ganesh Mantra and other daily mantras.

Aarti — Shiv Aarti, Hanuman Aarti, Ganesh Aarti, Lakshmi Aarti and more.

Our Values

This site is built with respect and devotion. We do not modify, alter or translate prayers in a way that changes their original meaning. All content is presented as faithfully as possible to the original texts.

The site is free to use and will always remain free. We display minimal, non-intrusive advertisements to cover the small cost of running the website.
